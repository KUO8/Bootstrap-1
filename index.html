About plants

Plants are the most perfect works of the great artist, who is called nature. There are a lot of them, much more in the number of species than animals, and in total more than all living beings on Earth. And yet the plants are so different. These can be beautiful proud flowers that adorn our gardens or parks, and these can be majestic multi-meter trees, the top of which cannot be seen even with your head thrown back. Plants grow all over the planet, high in the mountains and deep in the seas, because algae are also plants. Plants are diverse and at the same time they are all somewhat similar to each other. It may be that it is plants that give a person a lot of oxygen necessary for breathing.
